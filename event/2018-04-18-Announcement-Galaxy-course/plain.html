Galaxy HTS data analysis workshop in Freiburg

From 2018-09-17 to 2018-09-21 - Add to your Calendar

Galaxy workshop on HTS data analysis, September 2018, registration is open! For attendance, please apply here

The Freiburg Galaxy Team and Deep-Sequencing unit at the MPI-IE Freiburg offer an introductory course to the analysis of high-throughput sequencing data using the Galaxy platform.

Venue

Location

Institute for Biology II/III

79104, Freiburg im Breisgau,
Baden-Württemberg,
Germany

Important notes

  1. If you are registered but cannot attend our workshop, please contact us by email immediately. We have a long waiting list and can give your place to others, even on short notice.
  2. Please register at https://usegalaxy.eu if you have not done so already. For the MPI people: the European Galaxy server is different from the Galaxy server of MPI: you will need an account on our server, too.
  3. You can bring your own notebook or computers will be available. Eduroam is available, ask your institute for how to login.
  4. The workshop is free of charge. No stipends for travel or accommodation are available.

Preparation

You must go through two Galaxy interactive tours before beginning the training. These interactive tours guide you stepwise through the Galaxy user interface and the history. They will help you to follow the Galaxy introduction, and ensure everyone has a basic understanding of how Galaxy works.

  • Galaxy UI
  • History Introduction

Travel Options

From Basel airport to Freiburg you have two options:

  1. Take the train to Freiburg Hauptbahnhof (ICE or EC takes 30 min; Regio 60 min)
  2. Take the airport shuttle bus to Freiburg Hauptbahnhof (Timetable)

From Freiburg Hauptbahnhof to Venue you should use local public transport (VAG):

  • Tram line: 2
  • Tram stop: “Hauptstraße”
