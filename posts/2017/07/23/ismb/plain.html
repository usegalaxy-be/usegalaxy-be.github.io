Talks, poster and training @ ISMB and BOSC

»
Jul 23, 2017
Prague, Czech Republic
training talk poster

Björn and Bérénice were participating to ISBM/ECCB 2017 and particularly to Bioinformatics Open Source Conference (BOSC)) (satellite of ISBM/ECCB):

  • On 21st, they gave with Martin Cech and John Chilton a workshop “Making Galaxy Work for You”

  • On 22nd, Björn presented “The backbone of research reproducibility: sustainable and flexible tool deployment”

  • On 23rd, Bérénice gave a talk “Building an open, collaborative, online infrastructure for bioinformatics training” and presented a poster “Scaling Bioinformatics Training: An ELIXIR, GOBLET & Galaxy Training Network Collaboration”
