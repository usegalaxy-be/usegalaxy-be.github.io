End of our 5-day HTS Galaxy workshop in Freiburg, Germany

»
Sep 22, 2017
Freiburg, Germany
training

After 5 days, our HTS Galaxy workshop in Freiburg is over.

Final day of our #usegalaxy workshop at @UniFreiburg. pic.twitter.com/IQX8hjYha1

— Björn Grüning (@bjoerngruening) September 22, 2017

During this workshop, attendees learned:

  • Day 1: Galaxy and how to use it to analyze biological HTS data
  • Day 2: ChIP-seq
  • Day 3: RNA-Seq
  • Day 4: Exome-seq, MethylC-seq
  • Day 5: Recap and exercises

This workshop was organized by the Bioinformatics and Deep-Sequencing Unit at the MPI-IE Freiburg and our team.
