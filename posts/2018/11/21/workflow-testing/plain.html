Automatic workflow testing

»
Nov 21, 2018
Freiburg, Germany
devops

We have started to test a few of our workflows automatically against UseGalaxy.eu. This will run all workflows monthly to ensure that the keep working and that our infrastructure works and scales well.

Feel free to contribute workflows to our testing repository or propose us workflows via opening an issue. Alternatively you can write us at contact@usegalaxy.eu. If you would like us to test these workflows on your server, just get in contact with us and we can do that as well.
