A successful Galaxy RNA-seq workshop in Belgrade

»
Jan 19, 2019
Belgrade, Serbia
training

Last week, the Freiburg Galaxy team taught a 2 day course in the beautiful city of Belgrade!

This 2 day workshop was organized and hosted by the Institute of Nuclear Sciences Vinča in Belgrade. Björn, Helena and Bérénice offered an introductory course to Galaxy and RNA-seq data analysis for the 50 participants.

Based on the nice feedback we received, all of the participants seem to have enjoyed this workshop.

Thanks Nevena and Vlada for the invitation and the sightseeing ❤️!
